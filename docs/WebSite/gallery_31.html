Large-Eddy Simulation of Raleigh-Taylor Instability

The visualization to the left depicts a Large-Eddy Simulation of Raleigh-Taylor instability at an Atwood number of 1/2. The simulation was conducted on a 720x720x1620 point grid using 1620 processors, taking 20 days and generating 20 terabytes of data.
